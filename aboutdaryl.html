About Daryl Love

Daryl’s service on behalf of students began in 2001, when he became a proud and active member of the PTA at his children’s school. He later served as a member of the School Based Decision Making Council (SBDM). He was appointed to the Fayette County School Board in 2010, and elected in 2012.

Daryl has a genuine love for his community, as evidenced by his work with numerous organizations, including: the Partnership for Successful Schools, a statewide education advocacy group; the Governors School for the Arts; Hospice of the Bluegrass (Board Chair); the United Way and Commerce Lexington. He also was recently appointed to the St. Joseph Hospital Foundation Board of Directors.

Daryl Love, is manager of community relations and employee engagement at Ashland Inc., a global specialty chemicals company. He’s responsible for managing Ashland’s corporate contributions, community relations and employee engagement programs.

Daryl joined Ashland Inc. in 1988, as a computer systems analyst. Since that time he has held the following roles: senior systems engineer in information technology, senior compensation specialist in the corporate human resources group, international compensation and benefits specialist, manager, global benefits planning and HR services, and vice president, Equal Opportunity Finance, Inc., a subsidiary of Ashland Inc. that provided management assistance and counsel to women and minority-owned businesses. He joined his current department, Communications and Corporate Affairs in 2004.

A native of Memphis, Daryl graduated from Lane College, in Jackson, Tenn., where he received a bachelor’s degree in computer science and mathematics. He received his MBA from Midway College. He is an active member of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., and is also involved with his church, Main Street Baptist Church.

He and his wife Rhonda Love, have been married for 25 years and they have two sons.

Daryl Love is committed to helping students learn and become the best they can be. He is passionate about education and has a proven record of listening, collaborating and leading.

These unparalleled attributes make Daryl a valuable asset to the Fayette County Board of Education, where he has tirelessly represented the fifth district since 2010.

Daryl’s service on behalf of students began in 2001, when he became a proud and active member of the PTA at his children’s school. He later served as a member of the School Based Decision Making Council (SBDM) as well.

Daryl has a genuine love for his community, as evidenced by his work with numerous organizations, including: the Partnership for Successful Schools, a statewide education advocacy group; the Governors School for the Arts; Hospice of the Bluegrass (Board Chair); the United Way and Commerce Lexington. He also was recently appointed to the St. Joseph Hospital Foundation Board of Directors.

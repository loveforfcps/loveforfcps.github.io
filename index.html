Daryl Love has a passion for listening, collaborating and leading and its these unparalleled attributes that make him an invaluable asset to the Fayette County Public Schools.

Daryl has served the Fayette County Public Schools as a board member in the 5th district since 2010, though first served on Parent Teacher Associations throughout his sons' schooling, has been a member of School-Based Decision Making Councils, the Governor's School for the Arts advisory council and the Partnership for Successful Schools.
